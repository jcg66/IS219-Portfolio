## Problem

Alert fatigue makes security operations slow and noisy. Important signals get buried inside repetitive telemetry.

## Solution

I linked logs to NVD-style threat intelligence through a RAG workflow backed by Qdrant, with local inference constrained to 8GB RAM.

## Key Features

- Hybrid-local architecture tuned for low-memory environments.
- Queryable evidence retrieval for faster analyst decisions.
- Automated testing coverage for the retrieval and orchestration layers.

## Outcome

The project demonstrates practical AI orchestration without relying on a heavy backend and includes explicit automated testing evidence.
